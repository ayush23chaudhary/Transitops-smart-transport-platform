# Analytics Definitions & Calculations

This document details the mathematical models and logic for the analytics dashboard calculations, preventing fake data implementations.

## 1. Fleet Utilization
Fleet utilization measures the operational activity level of the registered active vehicles over a given period.

$$\text{Fleet Utilization (\%)} = \left( \frac{\text{Vehicles currently ON\_TRIP}}{\text{Total active registered Vehicles}} \right) \times 100$$

- **Numerator**: Count of `Vehicle` records where `status = ON_TRIP` and `isActive = true`.
- **Denominator**: Total count of `Vehicle` records where `isActive = true` (excludes `RETIRED`).

---

## 2. Fuel Efficiency
Measures the distance traveled per unit of fuel consumed for a vehicle or across the fleet.

$$\text{Fuel Efficiency} = \frac{\sum \text{Trip.actualDistance}}{\sum \text{FuelLog.liters}}$$

- **Trip.actualDistance**: Pulled from completed Trips associated with the vehicle.
- **FuelLog.liters**: Fuel logs logged during the same period.

---

## 3. Operational Cost
The absolute operating expenses incurred by running the fleet.

$$\text{Operational Cost} = \sum \text{FuelLog.cost} + \sum \text{MaintenanceRecord.cost} + \sum \text{Expense.amount}$$

*Note: Fuel log costs must not be logged in the Expense table, and maintenance costs must be drawn exclusively from MaintenanceRecord to avoid double counting.*

---

## 4. Attributed Vehicle Revenue
Calculated from the completed trips executed by a specific vehicle.

$$\text{Attributed Revenue} = \sum \text{Trip.revenue} \quad \text{where status = COMPLETED}$$

---

## 5. Vehicle ROI (Return on Investment)
Evaluates the financial returns generated by a specific vehicle relative to its initial acquisition cost.

$$\text{Vehicle ROI (\%)} = \left( \frac{\text{Attributed Revenue} - \text{Operating Cost}}{\text{Acquisition Cost}} \right) \times 100$$

- **Operating Cost**: Sum of fuel, maintenance, and expenses attributed specifically to that vehicle's ID.
- **Acquisition Cost**: The initial vehicle purchase price (`Vehicle.acquisitionCost`).
- **Attributed Revenue**: Sum of `Trip.revenue` for completed trips made by this vehicle.
